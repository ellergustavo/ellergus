Inglês

Carlos Alberto

Possui graduação em Letras, mestre em Letras, área de concentração Estudos Linguísticos, da Universidade Federal de Mato Grosso do Sul, Campus de Três Lagoas, nível Mestrado. Atualmente é Professor Universitário do CEETEPS - FATEC - nas modalidades de Ensino Presencial e Ensino à Distância EaD. Professor de Língua Inglesa I, III e V na Fundação Educacional de Fernandópolis - FEF.

Tem experiência na área de Letras, com ênfase no ensino de Língua Inglesa, Lingua Inglesa: Prática Oral, Inglês Instrumental e Linguística. No ano de 2009 trabalhou com tutor on line Casa Thomas Jefferson. É fluente na Língua Inglesa.

About translated

 I have a degree in computer technology from ETEC - Jales Unit. Also trained in English and Spanish by the Center for Language Studies. General study at the Faculty of Technology of Jales (FATEC), in the course of Systems for Internet. I try to participate in most of the extra activities of the institution, such as mini-courses, lectures and projects such as the Click More Age project, aimed at the digital inclusion of the elderly, guided by professors Carlos Alberto and Fabiana Caravieri; and also the hardware project, led by Professor Jefferson Passerini that aims to repair computers donated to the institution to be distributed to the community.

 I am passionate about technology and sports in general. I have hobby watching movies and series besides listening to music, of which I have a quite varied taste.

 I have the professional goals of the Doctor in an area of Computer Science and also become a teacher, because I am not the reader for the institution.
